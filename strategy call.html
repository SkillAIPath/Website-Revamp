Strategy Call FAQ

What exactly happens during the strategy call? +

We'll discuss your current situation, career goals, timeline, and create a personalized learning roadmap. You'll get expert advice on which track suits you best and how to achieve your objectives efficiently.

Is this really free? What's the catch? +

Yes, it's completely free with no obligations. We provide genuine value to help you make informed decisions about your career. There's no pressure to enroll - our goal is to help you succeed regardless.

Who will I be speaking with? +

You'll speak with our founder Viresh Gendle or senior team members who have real industry experience building enterprise data systems. No sales representatives - only technical experts.

What if I'm a complete beginner? +

Perfect! We specialize in helping beginners. Many of our successful students started with zero technical background. We'll assess where you are and create a plan that works for your starting point.

How soon can I expect results after the call? +

You'll receive immediate clarity during the call and a detailed action plan within 24 hours. If you decide to join our program, you can start seeing progress within the first few weeks of training.
