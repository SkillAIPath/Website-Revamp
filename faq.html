Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our AI & Data Science training programs, enrollment process, and career support

Course Information

What courses do you offer? +

We offer three comprehensive tracks: Foundation Track (skills & portfolio building), Professional Track (freelancing focus with ₹20K-40K/month guarantee), and Complete Track (full-time job placement with 6-15 LPA guarantee). Each track covers enterprise-grade tools like Azure Cloud, Python, SQL, Power BI, and Machine Learning.

How long are the courses? +

Course duration varies by track. Foundation Track is 3-4 months, Professional Track is 4-6 months, and Complete Track is 6-8 months. All courses include lifetime access to materials and ongoing mentorship support.

Do I need prior experience? +

No prior experience required! We've successfully trained students from diverse backgrounds including B.A. students with zero tech knowledge, B.Com graduates, and marketing professionals. Our curriculum starts from basics and builds up to advanced enterprise skills.

What makes this different from other courses? +

We focus on real business problems using enterprise-grade tools, not toy projects. You'll work with actual production systems, get hands-on experience with Fortune 500 company tools, and receive mentorship from someone who's built scalable data systems in real business environments.

Enrollment & Payment

How much do courses cost? +

Course fees vary by track and are designed to be affordable with EMI options available at 0% interest for qualified participants. Contact us for detailed pricing information and payment plans that work for your budget.

Do you offer EMI options? +

Yes! We offer 0% interest EMI options for qualified participants. Payment can be made via bank transfer, UPI, credit/debit cards, or other approved methods. We believe financial constraints shouldn't stop your learning journey.

What is your refund policy? +

We offer a 7-day cooling-off period with 100% refund, no questions asked. After that, partial refunds are available based on course completion percentage with valid reasons. See our detailed Refund Policy for complete terms.

How do I enroll? +

Start with a free strategy call to discuss your goals and choose the right track. After enrollment confirmation and payment, you'll receive course access within 24-48 hours along with onboarding materials and community access.

Learning Experience

Is this online or offline? +

Our courses are delivered online with live interactive sessions, recorded content for lifetime access, and hands-on projects. You can learn from anywhere while getting personalized mentorship and community support.

What if I miss a live session? +

All live sessions are recorded and available for lifetime access. While attendance is encouraged for maximum benefit, you won't miss out on any content. Additional support is available through mentorship sessions and community discussions.

What kind of support do I get? +

You receive comprehensive support including personalized mentorship, project reviews, career guidance, resume assistance, interview preparation, and access to our student community. Our goal is your success, not just course completion.

What tools and software will I use? +

You'll work with enterprise-grade tools including Azure Cloud, Python, Databricks, SQL, Power BI, ChatGPT API, DevOps tools, GitHub, VS Code, N8N Automation, Machine Learning frameworks, and more. We provide access to professional platforms used by Fortune 500 companies.

Career Support

Do you guarantee job placement? +

Our Complete Track includes a full-time job placement guarantee (6-15 LPA) for students who complete all requirements including 80% of coursework, maintain 80% attendance, and actively participate in career support activities.

What about freelancing opportunities? +

Our Professional Track includes a first freelance gig guarantee (₹20K-40K/month). We help you build a strong portfolio, connect with clients, and develop the business skills needed for successful freelancing in AI and data science.

What companies do your students work at? +

Our alumni work across various industries including tech companies, startups, financial services, consulting firms, and as independent freelancers. We focus on building skills that are in demand across the market, not just specific companies.

How long does it take to get a job after completion? +

Timeline varies based on individual effort, market conditions, and career goals. With our Complete Track guarantee and active career support, most dedicated students secure opportunities within 3-6 months of course completion.

Technical Requirements

What equipment do I need? +

You need a computer/laptop with stable internet connection. Basic software requirements will be covered during onboarding. We'll guide you through setting up your development environment and accessing cloud platforms.

Do I need to purchase expensive software? +

No! We provide access to enterprise platforms that would normally be expensive. This includes cloud computing credits, professional tool licenses, and development environments. Part of our value is giving you access to real enterprise infrastructure.

Can I use a Mac or do I need Windows? +

Both Mac and Windows work fine. Most of our work is done through web browsers and cloud platforms, so operating system doesn't matter. We'll help you set up your specific environment during the first week.

Certificates & Recognition

Do I get a certificate? +

Yes! Upon completion of 80% of assigned projects and meeting requirements, you receive a completion certificate. More importantly, you'll have a strong portfolio of real projects that demonstrate your skills to employers.

Are certificates recognized by employers? +

While certificates help, employers value practical skills more. Our focus is on building a portfolio of real projects and demonstrable skills that speak for themselves in interviews and job applications.

What happens after I complete the course? +

You join our alumni network with ongoing support, career guidance, and access to new opportunities. We maintain long-term relationships with our students and continue supporting your career growth beyond course completion.
