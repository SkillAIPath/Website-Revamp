Frequently Asked Questions

What if I have no prior experience in AI or Data Science? +

Perfect! Our Foundation Track is designed for complete beginners. We start with basic concepts and gradually build up to enterprise-level skills. Many of our most successful students started with zero technical background.

How much time do I need to commit weekly? +

We recommend 8-12 hours per week for optimal results. This includes live sessions, project work, and self-study. Our flexible schedule accommodates working professionals and students.

Is this course suitable for working professionals? +

Absolutely! Our evening session timings and weekend workshops are designed for working professionals. You can learn from anywhere while getting personalized mentorship and community support.

What if I miss a live session? +

All live sessions are recorded and available for lifetime access. While attendance is encouraged for maximum benefit, you won't miss out on any content. Additional support is available through mentorship sessions.

Do you guarantee job placement? +

Our Mastery Track includes career support and portfolio development to maximize your job prospects. While we can't guarantee specific placements, our track record shows 80%+ of students land relevant roles within 6 months.

What tools and software will I use? +

You'll work with enterprise-grade tools including Azure Cloud, Python, Databricks, SQL, Power BI, ChatGPT API, DevOps tools, GitHub, VS Code, N8N Automation, Machine Learning frameworks, and more.
