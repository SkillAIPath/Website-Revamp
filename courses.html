Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our training programs and enrollment process

Which track should I choose - Foundation or Mastery? +

Choose Foundation (₹55,400) if you're starting your data science journey or need core skills. Choose Mastery (₹1,50,000) if you want advanced skills in AI, MLOps, and enterprise-level expertise. Book a free strategy call for personalized recommendations.

What is the V1-V2-V3 learning methodology? +

V1 (Learn): Comprehensive training sessions. V2 (Practice): Hands-on practice and assessments. V3 (Present): Present your work to real clients or moderators. This ensures you not only learn but can confidently demonstrate your skills professionally.

Are EduFin loans really available? +

Yes! We have partnered with EduFin to offer education loans with easy EMI options. For the Foundation track, you can start with EMIs as low as ₹4,600/month. Loan approval is subject to EduFin's terms and conditions.

What enterprise tools will I get access to? +

You'll get hands-on access to Azure Cloud, Databricks, Power BI, SQL databases, Python/PySpark environments, and more. These are the same tools used by Fortune 500 companies, giving you real enterprise experience.

How flexible is the 5-12 month timeline for Foundation track? +

Very flexible! You can complete the Foundation track in 5 months with intensive study, or take up to 12 months for a more relaxed pace. The program adapts to your schedule and learning speed while maintaining quality standards.

Do I need prior programming experience? +

No prior experience required for the Foundation track. We start from basics and build up to advanced concepts. The Mastery track assumes you have foundation-level knowledge or equivalent experience.
