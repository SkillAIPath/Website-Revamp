Refund Policy

Quick Summary

7-day no-questions refund: Changed your mind? Get 100% of your money back within the first 7 days, no questions asked.

After 7 days: No refunds. This lets us allocate expensive resources and maintain quality. Use the 7-day window to evaluate the program.

Last Updated: January 15, 2025

Our Commitment to You

At SkillAI Path (operated by KRISHNAV TEKNOLZY PRIVATE LIMITED), we stand behind the quality of our training programs. This refund policy is simple: try the program risk-free for 7 days.

Refund Policy - Simple & Clear

7-Day Money-Back Guarantee

Within 7 Days of Enrollment:

  • Refund Amount: 100% of fees paid
  • Conditions: No questions asked, no explanations needed
  • Processing Time: 3-5 business days
  • Course Access: Revoked immediately upon refund approval

After 7 Days

No Refunds Available

After the 7-day cooling-off period, all enrollment fees are non-refundable. This policy allows us to:

  • Maintain small batch sizes and quality mentorship
  • Provide enterprise platform access and resources
  • Ensure serious commitment from enrolled students
  • Allocate mentorship time effectively

Why No Partial Refunds?

We create a personalized roadmap for you to follow. Once you enroll, we invest significant upfront work mapping your learning path, allocating resources, and preparing your journey.

Our programs include:

  • Custom career roadmap tailored to your background and goals
  • Access to expensive enterprise platforms (Azure, Databricks, AWS)
  • Live mentorship sessions with limited capacity
  • Personalized project reviews and guidance
  • Lifetime access to all course materials and updates

These resources are allocated when you enroll. The 7-day window gives you ample time to evaluate if the program is right for you before committing.
