Course FAQs

Common questions about our training programs and enrollment process

Which track should I choose? +

Choose Foundation if you're new to data science, Professional if you want to freelance, and Complete if you're targeting full-time employment. Book a free strategy call to get personalized recommendations.

Do I need prior programming experience? +

No prior experience required. We start from basics and build up to advanced concepts. Our students come from diverse backgrounds including commerce, arts, and marketing.

How does the guarantee work? +

Each track has specific guarantee terms. You must complete 80% of coursework, maintain 80% attendance, and actively participate in career support. Detailed terms are provided upon enrollment.

What tools and software access do I get? +

We provide access to Azure Cloud, Databricks, Power BI, and other enterprise tools worth thousands of dollars. You'll work with the same platforms used by Fortune 500 companies.

Can I switch between tracks? +

Yes, you can upgrade to a higher track by paying the difference. Downgrades are considered on a case-by-case basis. Contact our support team to discuss your options.
