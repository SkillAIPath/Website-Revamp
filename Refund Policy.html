Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 15, 2025

Our Commitment to You

At SkillAI Path (operated by KRISHNAV TEKNOLZY PRIVATE LIMITED), we stand behind the quality of our training programs. This refund policy ensures fair treatment while protecting our ability to deliver exceptional educational experiences.

1. Refund Eligibility Overview

Refund eligibility depends on several factors including course completion percentage, reason for withdrawal, and timing of the request. We've designed our policy to be fair to both students and our educational mission.

Refund Eligibility Quick Reference

Course Progress Refund Percentage Processing Time Conditions
0-7 Days (Cooling Period) 100% 3-5 business days No conditions
0-25% Course Completion 75% 7-10 business days Valid reason required
25-50% Course Completion 50% 10-15 business days Documentation required
50%+ Course Completion No Refund - Completion certificate eligible

2. Cooling-Off Period (7 Days)

2.1 Full Refund Window

Duration: 7 calendar days from enrollment date
Refund Amount: 100% of fees paid
Processing Time: 3-5 business days
Conditions: No questions asked, regardless of course access

2.2 How to Request Cooling-Off Refund

  • Send email to tech@skillaipath.com with subject: "Cooling-Off Period Refund Request"
  • Include your enrollment details and preferred refund method
  • Refund will be processed within 3-5 business days
  • Course access will be revoked upon refund initiation

Pro Tip: We encourage new students to explore the first week's content and attend orientation sessions before making a withdrawal decision. Our student success team is available to address any initial concerns.

3. Partial Refunds (After 7 Days)

3.1 Refund Calculation Method

After the cooling-off period, refunds are calculated based on course completion percentage:

Completion Tracking Includes:

  • Video lessons watched (V1 recordings)
  • Live sessions attended (V2 interactive sessions)
  • Projects submitted (V3 independent challenges)
  • Assignments completed and reviewed
  • Mentorship sessions utilized

3.2 Valid Reasons for Partial Refunds

  • Medical Emergency: Student or immediate family health issues
  • Financial Hardship: Unexpected job loss or financial crisis
  • Course Mismatch: Significant misalignment with course description
  • Technical Issues: Persistent platform problems affecting learning
  • Relocation: Sudden relocation affecting course participation
  • Family Emergency: Serious family situations requiring immediate attention
